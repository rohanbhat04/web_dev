About Galaxy AI

The Galaxy S25 series, introduced at Galaxy Unpacked 2025, features an AI-driven operating system that offers a highly personalized and intuitive mobile experience. Highlights include the Now Brief feature, which customizes lock screen information based on user routines, and AI Select.

  1. AI-Driven OS: Personalized and intuitive mobile experience.
  2. Now Brief: Customizes lock screen information based on user routines.
  3. AI Select: Summarizes content and aids in creative tasks.
  4. Multimodal AI: Recognizes natural language, images, and text.
  5. Open Collaboration:Partners with Google and Qualcomm for enhanced AI features.
